How we

Work

Our logistics company provides foreign trade outsourcing and transport logistics services. Our managers will settle all foreign trade issues and organize international cargo transportation any product, including: of any complexity and by any type of transport, allowing Your organization should concentrate on running its own business. We will take over as the organization of a one-time delivery, and ensuring the uninterrupted supply of your business with goods, raw materials, equipment. We can deliver goods from abroad "turnkey": supplier search, conclusion of a contract (both for yourself and for the Client), currency transfer, certification, customs clearance, door-to-door delivery.

  • 01
    Transaction calculation

    The Client fills out an Application. Our Manager calculates the cost of transportation and related services.

  • 02
    Payment to the supplier

    We can take on all the hassle of concluding a foreign economic contract and implementing currency transfers to your Supplier.

  • 03
    Documentary support

    Our Manager prepares and submits to the Client a complete set of necessary documents for completed transportation and related services.

  • 04
    Signing the contract

    Our Manager prepares the Contract based on the Application. The signing of the Contract can be carried out using EDS

  • 05
    Transportation and Customs

    Our Manager monitors the performance of transportation and related services at all stages transportation and customs clearance (if included in the application), as well as advises. Client by completeness and correctness of registration of documents required for transportation (customs clearance).

Why choose us

Services

  1. We will outsource the functions of the foreign trade department of your company: Foreign trade - Foreign economic activity

    • Working with imported Suppliers/Buyers, namely: search for partners, maintaining negotiations on the terms of the transaction, preparation for the signing of foreign economic contracts
    • Management of the movement of finance in the implementation of foreign economic activity, which it is an important component of the logistics process and can significantly affect the final result. Participation at all stages of the financial route, making it optimal and correct
    • Cost and profit management for foreign economic operations.
    • Provision of foreign trade agent services. (agent for foreign economic activity)
    • Provision of Importer/Exporter services.

    We also provide cargo consolidation services in warehouses of partner agents in China, that allows you to save money by sending a large batch.

    We work on Air routes from Southeast Asia, North America and Europe.

    If you are looking for a more economical shipping option, but are not ready to wait for your goods, when delivering it by sea, try the Railway service from China, which you can find out about here or by calling us.

  2. The "Cargo pickup" service provides for the receipt of cargo and accompanying documents to it in one place.

    Professionally executed packaging and correctly selected packaging materials will protect the cargo from damage, loss and unauthorized access during transportation.

    • Cargo pickup day to day
    • Cargo delivery at night
    • Fence/Weekend delivery
    • A choice of packaging is offered:
      • Frame packaging (rigid)
      • Frameless packaging
      • Wrapping packaging

  3. Checking the condition of the cargo is necessary both before shipment and during direct receiving the cargo. We carry out the following types of survey services:

    • Cargo survey
      • Inspection of the quality of goods at the manufacturing company
      • Pre-loading inspection
      • Control of the loading process
      • The presence of a surveyor during cargo inspection (veterinary, customs, etc.)
      • Survey inspection during transshipment unloading of the container
    • Inspection of goods in stock
    • Technical inspection of cargo vehicles, containers, wagons
    • Examination of damage and loss of cargo
    • Marine survey (survey of marine vessels)
    • Control of commodity collateral for banks
    • Grain and feed cargo inspections in accordance with GAFTA regulations

  4. The client receives a complete set of documents for reporting. We can insure valuable and fragile goods from possible damage for the period of storage of goods. If necessary, we will provide individual security. We treat the cargo carefully and carefully in during the entire period of responsible storage

    • Round-the-clock security

    • Loading and unloading equipment

    • Qualified personnel
    • Sending the cargo to the addressee after storage without the participation of the client
    • Receiving and weighing of cargo
